# English Abstract

Yalan Hu is an undergraduate student majoring in Marine Resources and Environment at Nanjing Normal University. Her current research training centers on a review project about particle dry deposition, with emphasis on literature-based evidence synthesis, metadata auditing, parameterization comparison, and figure-centered scientific communication.

Rather than treating the work as a simple review, she has been building a structured observational evidence table that supports cross-study comparison, parameter standardization, and model-oriented interpretation. This process has strengthened her sensitivity to data quality, particle-size definitions, land-surface heterogeneity, and uncertainty in dry deposition parameterizations.

Her future interest is to extend this process-based perspective from particle deposition to broader land-atmosphere interactions, especially soil moisture feedbacks, regional climate diagnostics, and the role of land-surface states in shaping exchanges of matter, energy, and water.
